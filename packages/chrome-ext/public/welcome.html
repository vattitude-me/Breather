Welcome to Breather!

Your gentle break reminder companion is now installed. Let's get you set up.

1

Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle icon 🧩 in your Chrome toolbar (top-right)

2

Find Breather

Look for "Breather - Break Reminders" in the list

3

Click Pin

Click the pin icon next to Breather to pin it to your toolbar

4

Start Reminding

Click the Breather icon to set up your first break routine

💡 Pro Tip
Once pinned, you can quickly access Breather by clicking its icon in your toolbar, or use the keyboard shortcut shown in your extensions menu.
